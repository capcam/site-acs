Join the ACED Cohort

Thank you for your interest in joining the ACED Cohort. If you are

  • aged between 40 and 80,
  • have never had cancer, or completed any cancer treatment over a year ago, and
  • are willing to consider participating in early cancer detection research studies,

please complete our enquiry form below and a member of our team will contact you.

If you decide to join the ACED Cohort, you will be asked to complete a questionnaire and provide a blood and urine sample. You will then be assessed and contacted if you are eligible to participate in any further research studies on early cancer detection.

Please see our FAQ for more information about the ACED Cohort.
